Booking and Scheduling

We support office teams and field engineers to feel empowered in their roles with the right tools to do their jobs, efficiently.

We take the headache out of running multisite operations by supporting your team with reliable, easy-to-use software that joins the dots between the back office, the field, and industry data with ease.

  • Book appointments from anywhere in the world, directly, by phone, through portals, your website or third parties.
  • Maximise your capacity across regions, patches, and skill types.
  • Manage on-the-day jeopardy appointments across multiple contracts.
  • Multi-skilled workforces - supporting the scheduling of multiple job type’s i.e. Smart Metering or EV etc.
  • Schedule the right engineer for the job with confidence based on their skills and qualifications.

Field Force Management

We know it’s hard to manage so many moving parts on a daily basis, whether that’s people, parts or places.

We support you by helping to close the loop between the office and the field, making working from an office or remotely, from a van easier through contextual tools and support:

  • View the day in the life of an engineer across multiple touchpoints
  • View and set Rota’s and Schedules
  • Record and manage Skills and Qualifications so only qualified engineers are assigned to jobs
  • Keep in the loop with your installations with ‘Follow my Engineer’
  • Ensure your engineers are safe through digital safety checks

Vehicle Tracking

We make it easier to understand how your fleet moves and operates, making the most of your vehicle data, so you can understand how and where you need to support your fleet and operations, We help you do this through:

  • Real-time telematics, location, and trip information
  • Routing and Messaging
  • Fleet Maintenance through fault code analysis, usage-based maintenance and crash reconstruction
  • Reporting and Alerts for vehicle health, out of hours and activity
  • Driver performance, helping you understand your people and how they drive
  • Plug and play technology, reducing vehicle downtime for installation

Metering and Commissioning

We know that when it comes to commissioning a Smart Meter planned workflows and support to get the commissioning over the line when the engineer’s on-site matters.

Understanding the onsite status and providing remote support for your Smart Metering workforce from miles away is how we help you bridge the gap, through:

  • Real-Time visibility of the Onsite Status in the Commissioning Process
  • Support with Ad-hoc Interventions (Cross-Site Support)
  • Real-Time Servest Requests (USP Ingesting)
  • Informed workflows - Notifying Engineers of what they need to know and when. Ensuring a fully commissioned meter before leaving a property.
  • Integrated engineer and data workflows supporting the engineer to move on to their next job without endangering the commissioning process

Electric Vehicle Chargepoints

The rise of Electric Vehicles is seeing new challenges for the electrical installation industry, from the challenges of the increasing levels of demand to scale from a few installations to hundreds or thousands a month.

Alongside the installation, there are direct customer requests, industry-regulated processes, third-party notifications, and an ever-changing grant environment. We help you through:

  • Streamlining remote site surveys and digitised client communication
  • Digital installation workflows capturing all audit information seamlessly
  • Branded customer journeys, reflecting your or your customers brand
  • Making the complex third party paperwork easier to complete and taking the pain away with automated submissions
  • Intuitive back office with integrated invoicing and payments
