Careers

We’re building and growing a team of talented people to help us improve people's lives with the use of technology

Since 2017 we have grown from a start-up to an established industry-renowned end-to-end technology platform specialising in getting smarter energy solutions installed in the home. We’ve done this by bringing together talented people who’ve become a team and created some fantastic user solutions together.

The rollout of smarter energy relies upon innovative technology enabling people to safely deploy smart energy solutions at scale.

Our team members come from different backgrounds, with different skills and experiences giving us a diverse environment to innovate, collaborate, learn and grow together. And more importantly, have fun while we do it.

Combining code/development and design with solid operational expertise, delivering solutions that excel in practical live environments.

Our motto is to solve complex challenges with simple solutions, and we do that by living our values:

Innovate

Collaborate

Enjoy

Culture & Benefits

We look after our employees with benefits that matter to them

Work-life balance is at the heart of our business, we don’t have a culture of burnout and support our teams to have a life outside of work. Whether this is the flexibility to drop off your children at school, having your birthday off, or simply being able to enjoy a nice relaxed beer in our office bar after work, we’ve got you covered.

We offer career development through self-led learning plans supported by our management team so every colleague has the opportunity to learn, grow and progress at their own pace or simply stay and thrive in their current role because they love it.

Other opportunities for growth, working for a scaling business gives you the opportunity to get involved with more than just your role, we’re about offering life-changing futures.

Our Offices

Our home is ever-evolving, much like our tech!

Based in an old textile mill you’ll find the character of the old industrial era mixed with the new.

We actively invest in our environment to make sure it’s as enjoyable as possible when we spend time in the office.

Whether it’s our spacious desks, Barista Station, or our phone pod that takes your fancy, there's a little something for everyone to make hybrid working as enjoyable as it should be!

As well as a great space we’re also lucky to have free onsite car parking, taking the hassle out of commuting.
