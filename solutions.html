Reach

The fully integrated field services management solution

Designed for companies operating across physical sites, and operating field services and warehousing teams.

Having already enabled millions of home smarter energy home installations, Reach is a scalable solution that supports companies growing engineering operations through to already established enterprise-sized operators.

A Cloud Software as a Service Solution, that's globally accessible and modular. Giving you the flexibility to use the building blocks you need, and provides a solid foundation for your business to operate upon, wherever you operate.

All engineering businesses are different, the highly configurable modules allow you to support your own operations and appointments.

Giving you the peace of mind when working with third parties that you don’t have to sacrifice the operational efficiency, or customer and internal user experience you pride yourselves on delivering.

That’s why Reach is capable of providing you with an end-to-end solution or the pieces you need in between, with the option of integrating with third-party and worldwide platforms, to support you across the touchpoints you need most.

Charge

The platform for Smarter Energy Solutions for the home

Charge enables the rollout of smarter energy installations in the home. From Electric Vehicle Chargepoints to Solar, Heatpumps and Battery Storage.

Charge is supporting Electrical Engineering Companies with the technology to deal with the challenges of installing complex emerging technologies, helping them to operate optimally and focussing on more installations, less manual paperwork.

S2

Providing cross-site commissioning support

The SMETs 2 commissioning Platform that can work independently or as part of the end-to-end Reach Suite.

Providing cross-site commissioning support through our field team Mobile Application or a computer. Giving real-time visibility, dealing with real-time service requests.

Greater efficiencies and support in the field through integrated engineer and data workflows, gving engineers concise information reducing jeopardy in the commissioning process.

Locate

A fresh approach to Vehichle Tracking

Harvesting your vehicle telematics, location and trip information, along with supporting fleet information, Locate presents you with live accurate data within an intuitive custom portal.

Giving you the agility of a plug-and-play solution without having to compromise on the big data available to utilise within your fleet operations.

Enigma

The REC compliant Gas and Electic data flow management system

When combined with other modules or used alone, Enigma is the first platform of its kind to offer an end-to-end solution for data flow coordination.

Our proactive core logic means you can book meter exchanges without considering agent appointment flows, retrieve missing meter technical details and submit data flows automatically from the field.

Created with the end-user in mind, requiring a manage-by-exception-only approach; helping to provide greater efficiencies when running a monotonous operation.
