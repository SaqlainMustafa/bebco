Consulting

We help our clients to understand and define clear, actionable strategies to solve their challenges and move their business forwards into success

Our team has supported some of the fastest scaling engineering workforces in the UK to grow, scale and optimise their operations. Working closely with our clients to uncover the true areas of pain within their business, ensurng our recommendations, strategies and solutions are underpinned by a solid understanding of their business. Ensuring:

  • Strategic Objectives of the business and the project align
  • Solutions or actions can be undertaken, with knowledge of the peripheral impacts
  • There is a pathway for short, medium and long term success
  • Human Centred approach to helping our clients engage and bring their people and business forwards together
  • Any technology recommendations have an operational action

Innovation Projects

We enable organisations to approach innovation projects and grants with a focussed collaborative and curious approach. Supporting our clients to uncover the value within the challenge they are looking to resolve, to secure funding or create a future business model.

We do this by leading way whilst collaborating on the strategy, design and development process for our clients. It’s about more than just expertise; we bring passion, curiosity and experience to every project. Having built, operated and scaled multiple innovative solutions we support our clients with

  • Business Model Generation
  • Value Proposition Design
  • Product and Design Blueprint
  • Prototyping
  • User Experience Design

Bespoke Software Development

We specialise in building category-defining technology solutions.

Technology selection is one of the most important decisions a business can make. We help our clients understand what to build, how to operate it and how to manage risk along the way. As things change quickly in business so we deliberately take a strategic view in all decision making. Today’s technology might not be tomorrow’s so we develop future-proof services to produce long-term solutions

Working on work on a ‘Build, Operate, Transfer’ model, each project starts with a shared understanding of the end goal. We help our clients to operate their solution independently. We develop, deliver and help put the right team in place.

We’ve built our team focusing on extensive industry experience. This approach ensures we only deploy the very best solutions.
